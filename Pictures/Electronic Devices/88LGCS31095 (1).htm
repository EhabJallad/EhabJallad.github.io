Fast and simple, for daily computing

  • Features

    Features

    Designed for those who dream bigger

    The Chromebook S330 brings the best Chromebook has to offer—in a sleek, stylish, and secure laptop in Business Black. Enjoy your favorite multimedia content with crisp visuals in full high definition (1080p) in a 14” IPS Display. At only 20.8 mm and 1.5 kg, and with a full keyboard and spacious trackpad, this Chromebook is perfect for day-to-day computing, on or offline.

    Performance for years to come

    Capable of handling all your daily computing needs with ease, the Chromebook S330 delivers the best in performance. Enjoy powerful processing for daily tasks, DDR3 memory for effortless multitasking, eMMC storage (expandable via the SD card slot with 100 GB of cloud storage through Google Drive), quick boot-ups, fast software loading, and more. Optimized to improve every day, year after year, the Chromebook S330 was designed to grow with you and deliver performance for years to come.

    Built to connect us

    Full I/O support comes standard on the Chromebook S330 with USB-C, SD card, and USB 3.0 support allowing you to connect to your favorite devices. The Chromebook S330 also features powerful 2 x 2 AC Wi-Fi with Bluetooth 4.1, allowing you to connect up to two Bluetooth devices at the same time, with a 720p webcam with two 2W speaker systems, for crystal-clear video calls. Further, Chrome OS delivers a suite of collaborative apps and services perfect for group work or individual play.

    Everything Chrome

    No setup needed—just log in with your Google account to experience a new type of laptop for everything you love to do. The Chromebook S330 is easy to use, offers built-in virus protection, and keeps going with long battery life. Stay synced with your Android phones & tablets, and everywhere you’re signed in with your Google account, so your stuff stays with you even when your laptop doesn’t—all while keeping email, maps, docs, and pics safely stored either locally or in the cloud.

    Apps have arrived

    The latest Google Play Store apps for work or play aren’t just for your phone. Enjoy the best Google has to offer, standard on your Chromebook. With apps ready to go from the moment you log in, you can enjoy access to Google Play Music, Games, Movies, Books, YouTube, Google Photos, Google Maps, Google Hangouts, and more. Further, the Chromebook S330 is infinitely customizable to meet all of your daily needs—from working, creating, or entertaining, to just plain having fun.
